Assistant Headliner

Ever feel so frustrated about a company you love not adding a simple feature requested by many?
Ever feel so frustrated you say 'F#ck this, I'll do it myself!'?
Well, we did.
So, we created Headliner Assistant. A nifty piece of software that creates images and albums of headlines from your favourite RSS feeds and subreddits to your Google Photos, so you can assign them to your Google Nest Hub's photoframe feature.
